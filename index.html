North Acres Homeowners Residents Association (NAHA)

North Acres is a quiet, friendly neighborhood in northeast Austin, Texas, with large yards, a peaceful duckpond, and gorgeous trees. Children, chickens, and craft groups dot the neighborhood. Among other notable individuals, renowned artist Fidencio Duran and King of the Dobro Players Tom Swatzell have called North Acres home.

NAHA maintains this page as a billboard for the outside world. We also have NextDoor and a listserv for residents. Contact us at NorthAcresHA@gmail.com.
