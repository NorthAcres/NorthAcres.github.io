North Acres Homeowners Residents Association (NAHA)

North Acres is a quiet, friendly neighborhood in northeast Austin, Texas, with a peaceful pond and gorgeous trees. Among others, renowned artist Fidencio Duran and King of the Dobro Players Tom Swatzelll have called North Acres home.
